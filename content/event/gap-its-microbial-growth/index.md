This guest lecture was delivered as part of the **CoE MicroPlanet Global Ambassador Program (GAP)**, an outreach initiative by the Cluster of Excellence MicroPlanet at the University of Vienna. The session was delivered together with [Catalin Rusnac](https://www.linkedin.com/in/cata7in/) from Replifactory.

Around **200 students** joined in a hybrid format, and the session turned into a lively discussion about microbiology, research, and career paths in science.

## Topics Covered

- Microbial growth kinetics and the economics of cellular resource allocation
- Trade-offs in microbial metabolism: growth rate vs. yield, speed vs. efficiency
- Quantitative models for understanding microbial behavior
- Introduction to bioreactors and computational approaches in microbiology
- Research themes from MicroPlanet WP 7.1
