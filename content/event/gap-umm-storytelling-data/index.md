This session was delivered as part of the **CoE MicroPlanet Global Ambassador Program (GAP)** at Universitas Muhammadiyah Malang (UMM), with faculty members and researchers from several departments.

## Topics Covered

- What makes a compelling data story?
- Thinking visually: beyond pretty charts
- Common mistakes scientists make when presenting data
- Real-world examples of data visualization in research
- Choosing the right chart for your message

## Key Takeaways

- Good data visualization is about clarity
- Always start with your audience in mind
- Use visual hierarchy and layout to guide attention
- Tidy data and the grammar of graphics make your life easier
